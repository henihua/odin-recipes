Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 8 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 chicken bouillon
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb. penne
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water

Instructions

  1. Combine the tomato sauce, half and half, chicken broth, chicken bouillon, honey, hot sauce, and seasonings and set aside. Measure out additional ingredients before beginning.
  2. Begin boiling salted pasta water for the pasta. Once a boil is reached, cook to al dente according to package instructions. (Set a timer to avoid overcooking.) Drain once cooked and reserve Â½ cup pasta water. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce.
  3. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the combined tomato sauce mixture in splashes, stirring continuously. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce to a simmer. Let it simmer gently, uncovered, while the pasta cooks
  5. Gradually sprinkle in the Parmesan and mozzarella cheese over low heat until combined.
  6. Add the drained pasta and stir to combine. Add a splash of pasta water if you prefer to thin out the sauce slightly. Excess pasta water can be helpful for reheating leftovers as well
  7. Serve with Garlic Bread with Cheese
